Data & Privacy

  • Your API key is stored in chrome.storage.local — only on your device
  • Your score history is synced via chrome.storage.sync — tied to your Chrome profile, not our servers
  • No browsing data, order details, or personal info is ever sent to our servers
  • Carbon analysis prompts sent to Gemini contain only item names and categories — not your identity
